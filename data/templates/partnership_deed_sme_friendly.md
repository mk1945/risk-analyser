# Partnership Deed (SME-friendly)

1. Partners and Firm
1.1 Names and addresses
1.2 Firm name and principal place of business

2. Business and Objectives
2.1 Nature of business

3. Capital Contribution
3.1 Initial capital by each partner
3.2 Additional contributions

4. Profit and Loss Sharing
4.1 Sharing ratio
4.2 Drawings and limits

5. Roles and Authority
5.1 Day-to-day management
5.2 Banking and signing authority

6. Accounts and Audit
6.1 Books of accounts
6.2 Periodic reporting

7. Admission, Retirement, and Exit
7.1 Admission of new partner
7.2 Retirement/resignation
7.3 Buyout/valuation and settlement timeline

8. Non-Compete / Non-Solicitation (reasonable)
8.1 Prefer non-solicit + confidentiality over broad non-compete

9. Dispute Resolution
9.1 Governing law
9.2 Arbitration/courts and jurisdiction
