# SME-Friendly Service Agreement Template (Outline)

## 1. Parties
- Service Provider: [Name]
- Customer: [Name]

## 2. Scope of Services
- Clear deliverables
- Acceptance criteria
- Change request process

## 3. Fees and Payment
- Milestones
- Invoice cycle
- Late payment interest (reasonable)

## 4. Term and Termination
- Initial term
- Mutual termination with notice
- Termination for cause with cure period

## 5. Liability and Indemnity
- Liability cap
- Exclusion of indirect/consequential damages
- Indemnity limited to third-party claims

## 6. IP
- Pre-existing IP stays with owner
- Customer gets limited license as needed

## 7. Confidentiality
- Definition of confidential info
- Exceptions
- Return/destruction

## 8. Dispute Resolution
- Escalation → mediation → arbitration (optional)
- Neutral seat, clear jurisdiction
